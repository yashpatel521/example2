About the Artist

Yaroslav is a professional painter with over a decade of experience exploring the boundaries between traditional techniques and contemporary expression. His work primarily focuses on oil and acrylic paintings that capture moments of light and emotion.

Artist's Philosophy: Painting stories with light and color.

In his work, Yaroslav aims to create pieces that invite viewers to step into a moment and experience the emotions and atmosphere he felt when creating the piece. Each painting is a journey, both for him as the artist and for those who view the work.
