About Yaroslav

Painting stories with light and color.

Yaroslav is a painter focused on color, light, and texture—blending narrative realism with modern abstraction. Here he shares studio notes, techniques, and works‑in‑progress across oil and acrylic series.

With over a decade of experience exploring the boundaries between traditional techniques and contemporary expression, Yaroslav's work captures moments of light and emotion in both plein air and studio paintings.

My Journey

I began my artistic journey at a young age, studying classical techniques before developing my own unique style that combines narrative elements with abstract interpretations of light and color. My studio practice involves both plein air painting and more contemplative work in my studio.
