About Yaroslav

Painter focused on color, light, and texture—blending narrative realism with modern abstraction

My Journey

Hello, I'm Yaroslav, a professional painter with over a decade of experience exploring the boundaries between traditional techniques and contemporary expression. My work primarily focuses on oil and acrylic paintings that capture moments of light and emotion.

I began my artistic journey at a young age, studying classical techniques before developing my own unique style that combines narrative elements with abstract interpretations of light and color. My studio practice involves both plein air painting and more contemplative work in my studio.

Painting stories with light and color.

In my work, I aim to create pieces that invite viewers to step into a moment and experience the emotions and atmosphere I felt when creating the piece. Each painting is a journey, both for me as the artist and for those who view the work.

When I'm not painting, I enjoy teaching workshops, writing about art techniques, and mentoring emerging artists. I believe that art is a continuous conversation between the artist, the medium, and the viewer.
